# Account and Sign-In

Sign-in is optional in the released Teale apps.

You can still:

- load local models
- chat locally
- use live Teale Network models
- earn into the device wallet

without signing in first.

---

## What sign-in is for

Use sign-in when you want to:

- link multiple devices to the same human account
- view linked devices
- keep device and account identity connected
- use account-level wallet actions where they are available

## macOS flow

On macOS, the **Account** tab includes a single **Sign in** action that opens the configured auth window.

After sign-in, the app shows:

- signed-in state
- account identifiers such as email or phone when present
- linked devices on that account

## Windows flow

On Windows, the **Account** tab exposes the released entry points directly:

- GitHub
- Google
- SMS phone verification

After sign-in, the app shows:

- account identifiers
- providers linked to the account
- linked devices
- account-wallet controls

## Linked devices

The linked-device list is the human-account view of Teale devices, separate from the device wallet itself.

This is where you verify that multiple Macs or PCs are attached to the same account identity.

## Sign-out

You can sign out from the Account tab at any time. Device-wallet earnings and local model management continue to work even after sign-out.
