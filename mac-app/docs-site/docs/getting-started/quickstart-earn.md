# Quickstart: Earn

Use the released app to supply inference, earn availability credits while your device is ready, and collect the provider share when the network routes real requests to your machine.

---

## How earnings work

The released apps surface two earning modes:

- **Availability earnings** while a compatible model is loaded, online, and ready to serve.
- **Served inference earnings** when Teale actually routes completions through your device.

Served inference uses the released split:

- **95% to the provider**
- **5% network fee**

## Step by step

### 1. Load a model from Supply

The fastest path is the recommended model action. You can also choose a different model from the catalog.

### 2. Leave the device available

- Keep the app running.
- Stay on power if possible.
- Avoid unloading the active model.

### 3. Watch the rate in the app

- **Supply** shows the availability rate plus the served-inference split.
- **Wallet** shows your current device balance, USDC reference balance, active rate, and start time.

### 4. Export or send from the wallet

The released apps support:

- sending **Teale credits** from the device wallet
- exporting the ledger as CSV
- displaying the same balance in either Credits or USD view
